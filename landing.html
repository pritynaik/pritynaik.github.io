About Me

I'm a 4th year BBA student at Wilfrid Laurier University.
Avid Learner. User Centric. Idea Person.

Nice to e-meet you!

Hey, Hi, Hello! Welcome to my YouTube channel about me page.

As a self-motivated BBA student in the Lazaridis School of Business and Economics at Wilfrid Laurier University, I strive to create change at every corner. I hope to utilize my Marketing and Accounting concentration and Economics minor to strengthen my skills in analytics, project management, digital marketing, design, and as a consultant. I trust in teamwork, the latest technology, creativity, and intuition in order to create a meaningful impact.

I have the passion, skill set and professional background necessary to dive deep into projects. As an upbeat, self-motivated team player with excellent communication, I envision an exciting future in the industry. I like to think of the big picture, because it reminds me of the end goal, but I’m pragmatic in my day-to-day work and can take fast decisions on what needs to be done in the short term. Working in consulting and accounting related roles, I have experience managing multi-stakeholder processes and am self-motivated but able to adapt to different team structures. As a lifelong learner, servant leader, and purpose driven business student who is trying to make a dent in the world, I love being surrounded by great people and working on projects that have the chance to make a scaled impact.

If I'm not found on campus or at work, then you can catch me trying different kinds of things ( shameless plug to visit Personal Initatives page) . Alongside with that, I'm always trying unique vegetarian food dishes, watching the Raptors and dancing with my friends. My workday mornings begin with coffee and catching up on tech highlights by reading TechCrunch, Fast Company and Digital Magazine. On the other hand, my weekends start at noon followed by some Netflix and family time!
